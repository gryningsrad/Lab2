Which of the following words are "proper" words to use onboard a ship?

Wall Starboard Floor (as in "go to the 3rd floor") Kitchen

Correct! Starboard is the right side of the ship when looking forward, and is the only of the four words that are a "proper" shipping-word. Wall is called "bulkhead", Kitchen is called "galley" and Floor is called "deck"
